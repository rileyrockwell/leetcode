## advantages and disadvantages compared to arrays
-primary adv: can add or remove element in any position in O(1), req. reference node
-primary dis adv: no random access; must iterate (could require O(n) to access an element)
-adv: LL do not have fixed sizes
-dynamic array is resized when size exceeded (expensive); LL do not suffer from this, but have more overhead
